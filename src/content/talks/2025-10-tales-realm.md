Oral presentation of **TALES: Text Adventure Learning Environment Suite** — a progressive stress test of LLMs' baseline agentic capabilities, spanning 122 tasks across which 49 models were benchmarked.

Joint work with Xingdi "Eric" Yuan, Ziang Xiao, Prithviraj Ammanabrolu, and Marc-Alexandre Côté.
