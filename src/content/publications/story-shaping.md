excerpt: "Reward design for RL agents is hard when *how* a goal is achieved matters — common-sense behavior, specific preferences. Story Shaping helps agents infer and align their actions with tacit knowledge from exemplar stories, using knowledge graphs to generate intrinsic rewards based on similarity between agent actions and the story world."
